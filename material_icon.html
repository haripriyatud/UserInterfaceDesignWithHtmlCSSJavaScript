Cloud cloud
a visible mass of condensed watery vapour floating in the atmosphere, typically high above the general level of the ground

Different height ranges of cloud

  1. Low clouds Surface - 7,000 ft
  2. Medium clouds 7,000 - 17,000 ft
  3. High clouds 17,000 - 35,000 ft

Types of clouds

  • Stratus/strato: flat/layered and smooth
  • Cumulus/cumulo: heaped up/puffy, like cauliflower
  • Cirrus/cirro: high up/wispy
  • Alto: medium level
  • Nimbus/Nimbo: rain-bearing cloud
