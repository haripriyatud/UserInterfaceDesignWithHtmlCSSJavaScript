Article on flowers

Roses

The rose has always been a symbol of love, beauty, even war and politics. The varieties, the color and even the number of roses have symbolic meaning. The rose is particularly well known as the flower of love, especially the red rose..

Lilies

The lily flower symbolizes purity and refined beauty. Depending on the color or type, the lily flower can have different meanings..

Jasmine

Jasmine is a flower which is delicate, atmospheric and sweetly fragrant in nature, and looks gorgeous along with it's green leaves.
